# SEO Research Notes - 2026-05-25

## Goal

Expand Shipping Label Helper beyond generic "shipping label size" pages into high-intent troubleshooting pages for marketplace, shipping software, and printer-specific print failures.

## Evidence Summary

- Mercari Help Center confirms sellers may print labels in 8.5 x 11 inch or 4 x 6 inch formats, which supports pages around Mercari format choice and wrong-format printing.
- ShipStation Help documents label layout, printer workstation, paper size, and DPI as causes of labels printing too large or too small.
- Rollo support content emphasizes 4 x 6 label setup, driver/page-size matching, and correct browser or system print settings.
- Pirate Ship support and seller discussions repeatedly point to 4 x 6 label files, thermal-printer setup, and Letter-paper printing as common confusion points.
- Community/forum patterns across eBay, Etsy, Shopify, Mercari, and printer-specific searches repeat the same symptoms: label too small, label cut off, sideways thermal output, barcode not scanning, browser preview scaling, and paper-size mismatch.

## Page Decisions

Added English pages only. Spanish and Chinese translations should wait until Search Console confirms impressions/clicks, so the site avoids translated thin pages.

New pages target:

- Platform-specific format confusion: Mercari 4x6 vs 8.5x11.
- Platform-specific shrink symptoms: Mercari and Pirate Ship thermal-printer shrink.
- Shipping-software layout mismatch: ShipStation too small or too large.
- Printer-specific setup failures: Rollo, Zebra, DYMO 4XL.
- Cross-platform practical tasks: print 4x6 on a regular printer, convert Letter labels to 4x6 thermal safely.

## Claim Boundaries

- Do not claim carrier acceptance when the barcode is shrunk, clipped, blurry, folded, or covered with reflective tape.
- Do not claim official affiliation with Mercari, Pirate Ship, ShipStation, Rollo, Zebra, DYMO, USPS, UPS, FedEx, or DHL.
- Do not present conversion as postage generation. The site only helps preserve scale/layout for labels the user already has.
- Keep the first recommendation practical: choose the correct label format, print at 100% / Actual Size, match physical paper size, and test before paid postage.

## Post-Launch Monitoring

Watch Search Console for:

- Impressions on printer names plus "too small", "cut off", "4x6", "actual size".
- Queries containing "Mercari 4x6", "Mercari 8.5x11", "Pirate Ship label too small", "ShipStation label too small", "Rollo label too small", "DYMO 4XL too small".
- Pages with impressions but low CTR; rewrite title/description first.
- Pages with clicks but poor engagement; add page-specific quick action or tool preset.
